# Question Set Representation

- domain: Tobacco Use
- questionnaire: ASSIST_full_Substance Use

1. **Question**: In your life, have you ever used tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)? *(NON-MEDICAL USE ONLY)*
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_1
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
2. **Question**: In the past three months, how often have you used tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)? 
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_2
   - options:
     - Never
     - Once or twice
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
3. **Question**: During the past three months, how often have you had a strong desire or urge to use tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)?
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_3
   - options:
     - Never
     - Once or twice
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
4. **Question**: During the past three months, how often has your use of tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.) led to health, social, legal or financial problems?
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_4
   - options:
     - Never
     - Once or twice
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
5. **Question**: During the past three months, how often have you failed to do what was normally expected of you because of your use of tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)?
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_5
   - options:
     - Never
     - Once or twice
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
6. **Question**: Has a friend or relative or anyone else ever expressed concern about your use of tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)?
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_6
   - options:
     - No, never
     - Yes, but not in the past 3 months
     - Yes, in the past 3 months
7. **Question**: Have you ever tried and failed to control, cut down or stop using tobacco products (cigarettes, chewing tobacco, cigars, etc.)?
   - code: ASSIST_TOB_7
   - options:
     - No, never
     - Yes, but not in the past 3 months
     - Yes, in the past 3 months
