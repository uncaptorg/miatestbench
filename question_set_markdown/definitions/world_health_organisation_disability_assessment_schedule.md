# WHODAS 2.0 — WHO Disability Assessment Schedule (Full Title)

**Domain:** Social and Occupational Function  
**Population:** Adults

## Description
Full-title version of the WHODAS 2.0 (12-item). See whodas_20_12.md for full scoring detail. Used in specific program configurations requiring the full instrument name.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- 12 items across 6 ICF domains. Simple sum 0–48; higher = greater disability.
- **≥25** = moderate disability; **≥37** = severe/extreme disability.
