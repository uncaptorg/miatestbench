# EDE-Q — Eating Disorder Examination Questionnaire

**Domain:** Eating Behaviours and Body Image  
**Population:** Adults (16+); eating disorder populations

## Description
28-item self-report version of the Eating Disorder Examination interview. Four subscales: Restraint, Eating Concern, Shape Concern, Weight Concern. Items scored 0–6 (days over past 28 days or frequency rating).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Global Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1.99 | Subclinical | Monitor if eating concerns reported |
| 2.00–3.99 | Mild–Moderate clinical range | Eating disorder assessment; dietitian referral |
| ≥4.00 | Clinical range | Eating disorder treatment indicated |

- **Shape Concern and Weight Concern ≥4** + compensatory behaviours = high medical risk → immediate physical health review (electrolytes, ECG, weight).
- **Restraint subscale ≥4** in low-weight individuals = medical urgency.
- Track alongside BMI and physical health markers — score alone does not indicate medical acuity.
