# Children's Social Understanding Scale

**Domain:** Social Understanding / Theory of Mind  
**Population:** Children; EMPHN child summary questionnaire

## Description
Assesses a child's capacity to understand social situations, interpret others' emotions and intentions, and navigate social interactions. Used in developmental assessments.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Higher score = better social understanding
- Low social understanding + communication difficulties = autism assessment pathway
- Low social understanding without communication difficulty = social skills intervention

## Source
EMPHN child assessment battery — programme-specific implementation.
