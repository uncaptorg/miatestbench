# OpenArms Intake Questionnaire

**Domain:** General Mental Health (Intake)  
**Population:** Veterans, first responders, and family members; OpenArms program

## Description
Structured intake questionnaire for the OpenArms veteran and first responder mental health service. Covers demographic information, service history, presenting concerns, current mental health symptoms, and treatment preferences.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored — used to guide clinical triage and treatment matching. Key flags:
- **Active duty / recent discharge** = adjustment and transition difficulties common
- **Multiple deployments** = cumulative trauma burden; complex PTSD likely
- **Family member carer role** = secondary trauma and carer burden assessment
