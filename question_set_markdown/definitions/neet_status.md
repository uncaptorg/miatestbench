# NEET Status — Not in Education, Employment, or Training

**Domain:** Social / Vocational  
**Population:** Young people 15–24; Butterfly program

## Description
Identifies whether a young person is currently engaged in education, employment, or vocational training, and explores barriers to participation.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **NEET status** = significant social determinant of poor mental health; associated with depression, substance use, and social isolation
- Duration of NEET status increases risk — >6 months NEET = priority for vocational intervention
- Barriers to address: mental health symptoms, lack of qualifications, transport, carer responsibilities, discrimination
