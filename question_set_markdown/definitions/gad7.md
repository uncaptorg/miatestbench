# GAD-7 — Generalised Anxiety Disorder Scale (7-item)

**Domain:** Anxiety  
**Population:** Adults (18+); validated in adolescents 11+

## Description
7-item self-report measuring generalised anxiety severity over the past 2 weeks. Items scored 0–3 (Not at all → Nearly every day). Also has good sensitivity for panic disorder, social anxiety, and PTSD as a transdiagnostic screen.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Severity | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–4 | Minimal | No treatment indicated; monitor if chronic |
| 5–9 | Mild | Self-management strategies; review in 4 weeks |
| 10–14 | Moderate | Consider therapy (CBT); review in 2 weeks |
| 15–21 | Severe | Active treatment warranted; consider referral and/or medication |

- **≥10** = likely GAD; sensitivity 89%, specificity 82% for GAD diagnosis.
- **≥10** also identifies ~72% of panic disorder and ~74% of social anxiety cases.
- **Treatment response:** ≥5-point reduction = clinically significant change; ≤4 = remission.
