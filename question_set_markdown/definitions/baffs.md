# BAFFS — Brief Assessment of Functioning in Families and Schools

**Domain:** Functioning (Child/Youth)  
**Population:** Children and adolescents; parent and clinician-rated

## Description
Brief measure of functional impairment across family relationships, school performance/attendance, peer relationships, and leisure activities in children and young people.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Higher scores = greater functional impairment.
- School functioning impairment = school mental health liaison; educational support planning.
- Family functioning impairment = family therapy or parent management training referral.
- Used alongside symptom scales to assess real-world impact.

## Source
Child mental health functional assessment framework — program-specific implementation.
