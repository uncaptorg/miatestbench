# Family Mental Health History (Ageing)

**Domain:** Family History  
**Population:** Older adults; ageing program

## Description
Family psychiatric and medical history for older adult populations, with emphasis on dementia, late-onset depression, and suicide history.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **Family history of dementia** = elevated risk; monitor cognitive function; early neurological review
- **Family history of late-life depression** = increased clinical vigilance; proactive screening
- See family_mental_health_history.md for full detail.
