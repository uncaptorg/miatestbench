# Kessler Psychological Distress Scale (10-item)

**Domain:** Psychological Distress  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
Standard K10 as used in the Kildare program context. See k10.md for full scoring detail.
10-item non-specific psychological distress measure over past 4 weeks. Scored 1–5 per item (total 10–50).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Likely Status | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 10–19 | Likely well | No intervention needed |
| 20–24 | Likely mild disorder | Brief intervention / GP review |
| 25–29 | Likely moderate disorder | Mental health referral |
| 30–50 | Likely severe disorder | Urgent specialist review |

- **≥22** = Australian threshold for mental health referral.
