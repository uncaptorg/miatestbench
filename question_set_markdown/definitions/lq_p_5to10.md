# Life Quality Scale (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Quality of Life (Child)  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated

## Description
Parent-rated child quality of life measure covering physical wellbeing, emotional wellbeing, social relationships, school functioning, and family relationships.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Higher score = better quality of life.
- Low scores across multiple domains = comprehensive psychosocial assessment.
- School and social domain scores guide targeted intervention (e.g., school refusal, peer difficulties).

## Source
Child QoL program-specific implementation.
