# Physical Health — Fluid Intake Assessment

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Adults with eating disorders; Butterfly program

## Description
Assessment of fluid intake patterns, including restriction and excessive intake, in eating disorder contexts.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Pattern | Clinical Risk | Action |
|---|---|---|
| <1.0 L/day | Dehydration risk | Medical review; hydration education |
| 1.5–2.5 L/day | Normal range | No action |
| >3–4 L/day (compensatory) | Electrolyte dilution risk | Electrolytes review; purging assessment |
| >8 L/day | Polydipsia / water loading | Urgent medical review; hyponatraemia risk |

- Excessive fluid intake used to suppress hunger or artificially elevate weight before weigh-ins = clinical management strategy required.
