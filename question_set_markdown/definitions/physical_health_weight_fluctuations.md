# Physical Health — Weight Fluctuations

**Domain:** Physical Health / Eating Disorders  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Tracking of weight change over time — losses, gains, and cycling. Contextualised within eating disorder history and treatment phase.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Weight Change | Clinical Significance | Action |
|---|---|---|
| ≥5% loss in 1 month | Significant | Medical review; nutritional rehabilitation |
| ≥10% loss in 6 months | Clinically significant | Medical monitoring; intensive treatment |
| ≥20% below healthy weight | Medical emergency in ED | Inpatient consideration |
| Rapid cycling (>5 kg/month) | Purging cycle indicator | Purging assessment; electrolytes |

## Source
NICE Eating Disorders Guidelines (NG69). [nice.org.uk](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng69)
