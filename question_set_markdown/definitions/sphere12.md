# SPHERE-12

**Domain:** General Health  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
12-item self-report screening for Mental Health Condition (MHC) and Somatic/Fatigue Condition (SFC). Used in primary care as a rapid biopsychosocial screen.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Subscale ≥3 | Probable Condition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| MHC | Psychological disorder | Mental health assessment |
| SFC | Somatic/chronic fatigue | Medical review |
| Both | Comorbid presentation | Integrated care |
