# PedsQL — Pediatric Quality of Life Inventory (Adolescent 13–18)

**Domain:** Quality of Life  
**Population:** Adolescents 13–18 years; self-rated

## Description
Adolescent self-report PedsQL version. Same 4-domain structure as child version (physical, emotional, social, school functioning). See pedsql_child_812.md for full scoring detail.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Total score <70 = at-risk; <55 = significant impairment.
- Social functioning subscale particularly sensitive to peer relationship difficulties and school refusal in adolescents.

## Source
[pedsql.org](https://www.pedsql.org/) | Varni JW et al. (2001). *Med Care*, 39(8):800–812.
