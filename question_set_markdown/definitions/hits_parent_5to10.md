# HITS — Hurt, Insult, Threaten, Scream (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Domestic and Family Violence  
**Population:** Parents of children 5–10; IPV screening

## Description
4-item self-report screen for intimate partner violence experience in the past year. Items ask how often a partner physically hurts, insults, threatens, and screams. Scored 1–5 per item (1 = Never; 5 = Frequently); total 4–20.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 4–10 | Screen negative | Safety check at next review |
| ≥11 | Screen positive for IPV | Immediate safety assessment; mandatory reporting assessment; safety planning |

- **Any "hurt" item >1** = physical violence present → same-session safety assessment.
- Positive screen in parent of young children = mandatory reporting threshold assessment for child protection.
