# PC-PTSD-5 — Primary Care PTSD Screen for DSM-5

**Domain:** Post-Traumatic Stress  
**Population:** Adults; primary care and brief-contact settings

## Description
5-item initial PTSD screen for use where a brief but sensitive screen is needed before committing to a full assessment. First asks about trauma exposure, then 5 yes/no symptom items.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–2 | Screen negative | No immediate PTSD follow-up required |
| ≥3 | Screen positive | Proceed to PCL-5 or structured clinical interview |

- **≥3** = positive screen; sensitivity ~95%, specificity ~65%.
- Designed to maximise sensitivity — minimise missed cases in primary care.
- Any positive screen warrants trauma-informed care approach regardless of diagnosis.
