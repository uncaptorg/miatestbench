# IPAQ — International Physical Activity Questionnaire

**Domain:** Physical Activity  
**Population:** Adults (15–69)

## Description
Self-report measure of physical activity across transport, work, domestic/garden, and leisure domains over the past 7 days. Available in long (27-item) and short (7-item) formats. Scored in MET-minutes per week.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Level | MET-min/week | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| Low (inactive) | <600 | Physical activity counselling; GP referral |
| Moderate | 600–3000 | Encourage maintenance; note mental health benefit |
| High (active) | >3000 | Reinforce; explore barriers if declining |

- **WHO recommendation:** ≥150 min moderate-intensity or ≥75 min vigorous activity per week.
- Low physical activity + mental health diagnosis = priority for behavioural activation or exercise-as-medicine referral.
- Physical inactivity in psychosis associated with 20-year life expectancy reduction.
