# Physical Activity Assessment (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Physical Activity (Child)  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated

## Description
Parent-rated assessment of child's daily physical activity level, type, and barriers. Based on WHO/Australian physical activity guidelines for children.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Daily Activity | Recommendation | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| ≥60 min moderate-vigorous | Within guidelines | Reinforce |
| 30–59 min | Below guideline | Activity counselling; community sport referral |
| <30 min | Significantly inactive | Occupational therapy or community health referral |

- Physical inactivity in children associated with obesity, anxiety, ADHD symptoms, and poor academic performance.
- Barriers to activity (e.g., disability, social anxiety) = targeted intervention.

## Source
[Australian physical activity guidelines (DoH)](https://www.health.gov.au/topics/physical-activity-and-exercise/physical-activity-and-exercise-guidelines-for-all-australians)
