# EQ-5D-Y — EuroQol Youth (Clinician-rated)

**Domain:** Overall Health  
**Population:** Children and young people; clinician-rated version

## Description
Clinician-rated version of EQ-5D-Y. Used where child self-report is not feasible. See eq5dy_individual.md for full detail.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Clinician rates 5 dimensions on 3-level scale. VAS records clinician's assessment of patient's health state today.
- Discrepancy between child self-report and clinician rating = explore insight and collateral history.
