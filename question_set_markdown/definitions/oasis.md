# OASIS — Overall Anxiety Severity and Impairment Scale

**Domain:** Anxiety  
**Population:** Adults; transdiagnostic

## Description
5-item self-report measuring anxiety frequency, intensity, avoidance, and interference with work and social functioning. Transdiagnostic — valid across GAD, panic, social anxiety, specific phobia, and PTSD.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Severity | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–7 | Subclinical | Monitor |
| 8–11 | Mild | Brief intervention; self-help |
| 12–15 | Moderate | Therapy (CBT); review in 2 weeks |
| 16–19 | Severe | Active treatment; consider referral |
| 20–20 | Very Severe | Urgent review |

- **≥8** = positive screen; proceed to disorder-specific assessment.
- **≥4-point change** = clinically meaningful improvement.
