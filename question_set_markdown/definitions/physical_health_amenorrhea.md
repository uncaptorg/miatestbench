# Physical Health — Amenorrhea Assessment

**Domain:** Physical Health / Eating Disorders  
**Population:** Females with eating disorders; Butterfly program

## Description
Assessment of menstrual irregularity or cessation in the context of eating disorders. Secondary amenorrhea (absence of periods for ≥3 consecutive cycles) in the context of low weight or caloric restriction.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Finding | Clinical Significance | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Regular menstrual cycle | No amenorrhea concern | Continue monitoring |
| Irregular periods | Possible hypothalamic suppression | GP review; assess nutritional status |
| Secondary amenorrhea | Hypothalamic amenorrhea; medical urgency | Urgent medical review; bone density assessment |

- Amenorrhea + low weight = significant bone density loss risk (fracture risk increases substantially after 6+ months).
- Return of menstruation is a key physical health recovery marker in restrictive eating disorders.
