# Physical Health — Physical Activity (Butterfly)

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Adults with eating disorders; Butterfly program

## Description
Assessment of exercise frequency, intensity, compulsive exercise patterns, and exercise motivation in eating disorder context.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **Compulsive exercise** (unable to reduce, distress when prevented, exercise despite injury) = a maintaining factor in eating disorders requiring specific intervention
- **>2 hours structured exercise/day** in low-weight individual = medical review required
- Exercise restriction may be medically necessary at low BMI — clarify with treating team
