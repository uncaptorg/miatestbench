# ASSIST — Other Substance Use (Full)

**Domain:** Other Substance Use  
**Population:** Adults (16+)

## Description
WHO ASSIST modules for non-cannabis, non-alcohol, non-tobacco substances including cocaine, amphetamines, inhalants, sedatives, hallucinogens, opioids, and other drugs.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3 | Low | Brief advice |
| 4–26 | Moderate | Brief intervention |
| ≥27 | High | Specialist drug and alcohol referral |

- **Any injecting drug use** regardless of score = high risk; harm reduction + BBV testing.
