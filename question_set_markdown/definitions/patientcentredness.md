# Patient-Centredness Measure

**Domain:** Therapeutic Relationship / Care Quality  
**Population:** Adults

## Description
Assessment of the degree to which care is aligned with the client's own goals, values, and preferences. Measures goal concordance and therapeutic alliance quality.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Not numerically scored — qualitative review of goal alignment
- Low patient-centredness = review treatment goals collaboratively with client; motivational interviewing approach
- High discordance between client goals and treatment focus = treatment engagement risk; revise care plan
