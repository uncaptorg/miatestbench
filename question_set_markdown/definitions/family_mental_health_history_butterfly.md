# Family Mental Health History (Butterfly Program)

**Domain:** Family History  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly eating disorder program

## Description
Family mental health and eating disorder history for eating disorder presentations.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **Family history of eating disorders** = elevated genetic risk; assess family dynamics around food and weight
- **Family weight-focused comments or dieting culture** = maintaining factor; consider family therapy
- **Parental anxiety or perfectionism** = common in eating disorder family systems; family-based therapy consideration
