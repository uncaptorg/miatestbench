# Help-Seeking History

**Domain:** Treatment History  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Assessment of prior engagement with mental health and general health services, barriers to help-seeking, treatment preferences, and reasons for previous disengagement.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Clinically relevant patterns:
- **Multiple service contacts without benefit** = treatment-resistant presentation; review formulation
- **Disengagement due to stigma** = psychoeducation priority; destigmatisation approach
- **Cultural barriers** = culturally appropriate service matching; interpreter/cultural consultant
- **No previous help-seeking** = brief intervention and care navigation needed

## Source
Standard clinical history framework.
