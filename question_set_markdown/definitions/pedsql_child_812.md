# PedsQL — Pediatric Quality of Life Inventory (Child 8–12)

**Domain:** Quality of Life  
**Population:** Children 8–12 years; self-rated

## Description
Child self-report QoL measure covering 4 domains: physical functioning, emotional functioning, social functioning, and school functioning. 23 items rated 0–4 (Never → Almost Always), reverse-scored to 0–100.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Total Score | QoL Level | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| ≥70 | Near normative | Monitor |
| 55–69 | At-risk | Review each domain; family support plan |
| <55 | Significantly impaired | Priority intervention; school liaison |

- **Physical Summary <65 + Psychosocial Summary <65** = dual impairment → comprehensive care plan.
- School functioning subscale <65 = school mental health liaison recommended.
- Validated discriminant value: <69.7 distinguishes healthy vs. paediatric illness.
