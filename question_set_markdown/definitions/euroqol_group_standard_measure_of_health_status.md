# EQ-5D — EuroQol Group Standard Measure of Health Status (Adult)

**Domain:** Overall Health  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
Standard adult EQ-5D health status measure. 5 dimensions: mobility, self-care, usual activities, pain/discomfort, anxiety/depression (each rated 1–3 or 1–5 in EQ-5D-5L), plus EQ VAS (0–100).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Utility Index | Health State | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| 0.8–1.0 | Good health | Routine |
| 0.5–0.79 | Moderate impairment | Targeted intervention |
| <0.5 | Severe impairment | Priority case; multi-domain care plan |

- Widely used for health economic evaluation and quality-adjusted life year (QALY) calculations.
- Anxiety/depression dimension alone predicts mental health treatment need with >80% specificity.
