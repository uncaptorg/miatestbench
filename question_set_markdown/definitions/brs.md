# BRS — Brief Resilience Scale

**Domain:** Resilience  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
6-item self-report measuring the ability to bounce back from stress and adversity. Three positively worded and three negatively worded items, averaged to produce a score of 1–5.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Average Score | Resilience Level | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| 1.00–2.99 | Low resilience | Priority for resilience-building intervention |
| 3.00–4.30 | Normal resilience | Supportive; maintain coping resources |
| 4.31–5.00 | High resilience | Protective factor; reinforce strengths |

- **Low BRS predicts** slower recovery from depression, higher chronic pain impact, and poorer health behaviour outcomes.
- Use alongside coping style assessment — low BRS with external locus of control = intensive support.
