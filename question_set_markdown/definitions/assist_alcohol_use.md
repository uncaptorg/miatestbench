# ASSIST — Alcohol Involvement (Full)

**Domain:** Alcohol Use  
**Population:** Adults (16+)

## Description
WHO ASSIST (Alcohol, Smoking and Substance Involvement Screening Test) alcohol module. 8 questions covering lifetime use, past 3 months frequency, craving, problems, and injecting behaviour.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–10 | Low | Brief advice on risks |
| 11–26 | Moderate | Brief intervention (15–30 min) |
| ≥27 | High | Referral to specialist alcohol service |

- Injecting drug use at any score = automatic high risk.
