# NODS-CLIP — Brief Pathological Gambling Screen

**Domain:** Gambling  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
3-item brief screen derived from the NORC DSM Screen for Gambling Problems. Items assess Control loss, Lying about gambling, and Preoccupation with gambling — the three most diagnostically efficient items from the full NODS.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Low risk | Brief education if gambling mentioned |
| ≥1 | Screen positive | Full NODS or PGSI assessment; problem gambling brief intervention |

- **Sensitivity 0.99, specificity 0.91** for lifetime pathological gambling.
- Positive screen → refer to problem gambling counselling service (e.g., Gambler's Help).
- Gambling disorder often co-presents with depression, anxiety, and substance use — screen for all.
