# APA DSM-5 Level 1 Cross-Cutting Symptom Screen (Parent 6–17)

**Domain:** Transdiagnostic Child Mental Health  
**Population:** Children and adolescents 6–17; parent-rated

## Description
DSM-5 Level 1 cross-cutting screen covering 12 mental health domains in children: depression, anger/irritability, mania, anxiety, somatic symptoms, inattention, suicidal ideation/self-harm, psychosis, sleep problems, repetitive thoughts, substance use, and dissociation.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Domain Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 per domain | Not significant | No action for that domain |
| ≥2 on any domain | Positive screen for that domain | Proceed to Level 2 (domain-specific) assessment |

- Endorsement of suicidal ideation item = immediate C-SSRS or detailed risk assessment regardless of score.
- Multiple domain positives = comprehensive diagnostic assessment.
