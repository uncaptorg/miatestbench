# Physical Health — Pattern of Eating

**Domain:** Physical Health / Eating Disorders  
**Population:** Adults with eating disorders; Butterfly program

## Description
Structured assessment of meal frequency, meal skipping, binge-purge behaviour, compensatory behaviours, and food rules in eating disorder presentations.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored — item review identifies:
- Meal skipping + rigid food rules = restrictive eating → nutritional rehabilitation protocol
- Binge episodes → CBT-E for binge eating; medical monitoring for purging
- Post-meal compensatory behaviour (exercise, vomiting, laxatives) → assess medical risk and purging frequency
- Night eating syndrome → sleep-focused CBT; separate treatment pathway
