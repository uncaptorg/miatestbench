# PSQI — Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index

**Domain:** Sleep  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
19 self-rated items generating 7 component scores: subjective sleep quality, sleep latency, sleep duration, habitual sleep efficiency, sleep disturbances, use of sleeping medication, and daytime dysfunction. Covers the past month.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Global Score | Sleep Quality | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 | Good | No action; health promotion |
| 6–10 | Poor | Sleep hygiene intervention; rule out primary sleep disorder |
| 11–21 | Very Poor | Referral for sleep study or specialist review |

- **>5 = poor sleep** (sensitivity 98.7%, specificity 84.4%).
- **Component 6 elevated (sleep medication)** = dependence risk; review prescribing.
- **Component 5 elevated (disturbances)** = screen for sleep apnoea, RLS, nocturia.
- Poor sleep strongly associated with depression relapse and metabolic disorder.
