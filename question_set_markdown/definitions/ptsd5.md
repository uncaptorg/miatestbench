# PTSD-5 — Brief PTSD Screen (5-item)

**Domain:** Post-Traumatic Stress  
**Population:** Adults; Australian community mental health

## Description
5-item brief PTSD screen used in Australian community mental health settings. Covers intrusion, avoidance, negative mood, hyperarousal, and functional impairment.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–2 | Screen negative | Monitor if trauma history present |
| ≥3 | Screen positive | Full assessment (PCL-5 or CAPS); trauma-focused treatment pathway |

- **≥3** indicates probable PTSD requiring full assessment.
- Use alongside trauma-informed formulation — screen positives may have complex trauma presentation.
