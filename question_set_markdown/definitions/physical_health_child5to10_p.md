# Physical Health Assessment — Child (5–10, Parent)

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-reported

## Description
Structured parent-reported physical health assessment for children covering medical conditions, medications, sleep, appetite, physical development, and sensory concerns.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored — item review flags:
- Chronic medical conditions = GP coordination required
- Developmental delays = paediatric referral
- Sleep problems = sleep assessment (CSHQ)
- Sensory sensitivities = OT assessment

## Source
Standard paediatric health assessment framework.
