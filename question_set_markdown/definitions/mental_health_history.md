# Mental Health History

**Domain:** Mental Health History  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Structured mental health history covering previous diagnoses, psychiatric hospitalisations, medications trialled (and responses), self-harm history, trauma history, and current treatment engagement.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Key clinical review points:
- Previous psychiatric hospitalisations = establish triggers and recovery trajectory
- Medication history = avoid re-trialling ineffective agents; note side-effect profile
- Trauma history + current mental health = trauma-informed formulation mandatory
- Previous suicide attempt = strongest predictor of future attempt; explicit safety assessment

## Source
Standard clinical mental health history framework.
