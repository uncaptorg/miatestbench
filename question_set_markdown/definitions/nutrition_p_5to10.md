# Nutrition Assessment (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Physical Health / Nutrition (Child)  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated

## Description
Structured assessment of dietary patterns, meal regularity, food group coverage, and feeding behaviour in children. Used to identify nutritional risk and disordered eating presentations.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- No composite score — item review identifies:
  - Food restriction/selectivity → occupational therapy (sensory feeding difficulties) or dietitian referral
  - Binge eating/loss of control eating → eating disorder pathway
  - Poor food variety/growth concerns → paediatric dietitian
- Nutritional insufficiency affects cognitive function, mood, and physical development.
