# Screen Time Assessment (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Digital/Lifestyle Health (Child)  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated

## Description
Assessment of daily recreational screen time (TV, gaming, social media, devices) and its interference with sleep, physical activity, and social interaction.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Daily Screen Time | Recommendation | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| <1 hour (5–6 years) | Within guidelines | No concern |
| <2 hours (7–10 years) | Within guidelines | No concern |
| >2 hours | Above guidelines | Discuss family digital boundaries; sleep hygiene |
| >4 hours | Significantly elevated | Assess for gaming disorder (ICD-11); sleep and attention impact |

- High screen time + poor sleep + inattention = differential includes ADHD vs. behavioural sleep problem vs. gaming disorder.
