# QIDS — Quick Inventory of Depressive Symptomatology

**Domain:** Depressed Mood  
**Population:** Adults; also used in adolescents

## Description
16-item self-report (QIDS-SR) or clinician-rated (QIDS-C) covering all 9 DSM-5 MDD criterion symptom domains. Scores across sleep, mood, appetite/weight, concentration, self-view, suicidal ideation, interest, energy, and psychomotor symptoms.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Severity | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 | None | Monitor |
| 6–10 | Mild | Self-management; review |
| 11–15 | Moderate | Active treatment (therapy/medication) |
| 16–20 | Severe | Intensive treatment; consider augmentation |
| 21–27 | Very Severe | Urgent review; possible hospitalisation |

- **≥50% score reduction** = treatment response; **≤5** = remission.
- Preferred over PHQ-9 for tracking antidepressant trials due to broader symptom coverage.
