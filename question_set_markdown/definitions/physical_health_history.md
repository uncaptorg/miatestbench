# Physical Health History

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Structured history of current physical health conditions, medications, allergies, recent hospitalisations, surgeries, and GP contact. Used to establish a comprehensive physical health baseline.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Clinician reviews for:
- Medication interactions with proposed mental health treatments
- Physical health conditions that may contribute to mental health presentation (e.g., thyroid disease, chronic pain)
- Physical health monitoring requirements (e.g., lithium → renal/thyroid; clozapine → metabolic/haematological)

## Source
Standard clinical history-taking framework.
