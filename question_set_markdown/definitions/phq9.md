# PHQ-9 — Patient Health Questionnaire (9-item)

**Domain:** Depressed Mood  
**Population:** Adults (16+); also validated in adolescents

## Description
9-item self-report screening and severity measure for depression. Each item asks how often over the past 2 weeks the respondent was bothered by a symptom, scored 0 (Not at all) to 3 (Nearly every day). Item 9 directly screens for suicidal ideation.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Severity | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–4 | Minimal | Monitor; no treatment indicated unless clinician concern |
| 5–9 | Mild | Watchful waiting; review in 2–4 weeks |
| 10–14 | Moderate | Treatment plan (therapy and/or medication); follow up in 2 weeks |
| 15–19 | Moderately Severe | Active treatment recommended; consider antidepressants |
| 20–27 | Severe | Immediate treatment; consider referral to psychiatry |

- **Item 9 (suicidal ideation):** Any score >0 requires direct clinical assessment regardless of total score.
- **Treatment response:** ≥50% reduction = response; score ≤4 = remission.
