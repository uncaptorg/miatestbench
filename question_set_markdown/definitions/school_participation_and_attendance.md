# School Participation and Attendance

**Domain:** Education/Functioning (Child/Youth)  
**Population:** Children and adolescents; parent or clinician-rated

## Description
Structured assessment of school attendance percentage, engagement with learning, and barriers to participation.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Attendance | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| ≥90% | Satisfactory | Monitor |
| 80–89% | At-risk threshold | Brief school-clinician liaison |
| <80% | At-risk | School refusal assessment; coordinated plan |
| <50% | Significant disengagement | Multi-agency response; educational pathway planning |

- School avoidance often driven by anxiety, social difficulties, or bullying — clinically assess cause before intervention.
- Chronic absenteeism predicts NEET status, depression, and poor adult outcomes.
