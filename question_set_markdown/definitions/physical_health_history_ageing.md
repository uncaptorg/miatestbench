# Physical Health History (Ageing)

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Older adults; ageing program

## Description
Physical health history adapted for older adult populations, with emphasis on polypharmacy, falls risk, cognitive changes, and age-related conditions.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Key clinical flags:
- **Polypharmacy (≥5 medications)** = medication review required (risk of drug-drug interactions and falls)
- **Falls in past 12 months** = falls risk assessment
- **Cognitive complaints + physical health changes** = dementia screening (MMSE/MoCA)

## Source
Standard geriatric clinical assessment framework.
