# ReQoL-10 — Recovering Quality of Life (10-item)

**Domain:** Quality of Life  
**Population:** Adults with mental health conditions (16+)

## Description
10-item mental health-specific quality of life measure. Items cover activities, belonging, future, physical health, self-care, hope, and coping. Developed specifically for people with experience of mental health conditions. Scored 0–4 per item (0–40 total).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | QoL Level | Clinical Note |
|---|---|---|
| 0–15 | Significantly impaired QoL | Priority for QoL-focused intervention |
| 16–24 | Moderate QoL | Identify lowest-scoring domains; set QoL goals |
| 25–40 | Good QoL | Consolidation and maintenance |

- General population reference: ~34; mental health population mean: ~20–25.
- **≥5-point change** = minimally clinically important difference.
- Item-level analysis: items on hope and future orientation are particularly sensitive to recovery progress.
