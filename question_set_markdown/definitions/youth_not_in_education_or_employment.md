# Youth Not in Education or Employment

**Domain:** Social / Vocational  
**Population:** Young people; program-specific

## Description
Similar to NEET status assessment. Structured screen for educational and employment disengagement in young people, with exploration of barriers and aspirations.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Same clinical implications as neet_status.md.
- NEET + mental health + substance use = highest risk cluster for poor outcomes.
- Vocational/educational pathway planning is a core component of youth recovery goals.
