# AUDIT — Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test (Consumption / Full)

**Domain:** Alcohol Use  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
10-item WHO alcohol screen covering quantity/frequency of consumption (items 1–3), dependence symptoms (items 4–6), and alcohol-related harm (items 7–10). Items 1–3 alone form the AUDIT-C. Scored 0–4 per item (0–40 total).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–7 | Low risk | Health education; safe drinking information |
| 8–15 | Hazardous/risky use | Brief intervention (5–15 min counselling) |
| 16–19 | Harmful use | Brief counselling and monitoring; consider referral |
| ≥20 | Probable dependence | Specialist alcohol treatment referral |

- Items 4–6 elevated independently of total = dependence features present → medical review for withdrawal risk.
- Item 10 (CAGE-type) = concern for family/friends about drinking → motivational interviewing.
