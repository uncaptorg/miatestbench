# ASRM — Altman Self-Rating Mania Scale

**Domain:** Mania-Like Experiences  
**Population:** Adults (18+); bipolar disorder monitoring

## Description
5-item self-report measuring manic and hypomanic symptoms over the past week. Items cover elevated mood, increased motor activity, sexual interest, sleep, and irritability/speech. Each item scored 0–4.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 | Normal; no mania | Routine monitoring |
| ≥6 | Probable hypomanic or manic episode | Urgent clinical review; medication review |

- **≥6** = sensitivity 85.5%, specificity 87.3% for hypomanic/manic episode.
- Item 3 (sleep reduction without fatigue) is a high-specificity mania indicator.
- Rapid escalation over days = consider hospitalisation; assess risk of impulsive behaviour.
