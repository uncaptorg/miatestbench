# AQ-10 — Autism Spectrum Quotient 10-item (Parent, 5–10)

**Domain:** Autism Spectrum  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated version

## Description
10-item autism screening tool from the Autism Research Centre. Parent-rated version for children 5–10 assessing social skills, attention switching, communication, and imagination.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 | Below threshold | Monitor if clinical concern remains |
| ≥6 | Positive screen | Refer for formal autism diagnostic assessment (ADOS-2/ADI-R) |

- Sensitivity ~88%, specificity ~91% for autism at cut-off of 6.
- Positive screen = referral to paediatric developmental team or specialist autism assessment service.
- Do not diagnose autism from AQ-10 alone — comprehensive assessment required.

## Source
[autismresearchcentre.com](https://www.autismresearchcentre.com/arc_tests) | Allison C et al. (2012). *PLoS ONE*, 7(9):e44733.
