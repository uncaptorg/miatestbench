# ICG — Inventory of Complicated Grief

**Domain:** Grief and Loss  
**Population:** Adults bereaved ≥6 months

## Description
19-item self-report measuring prolonged grief disorder symptoms: intense yearning, difficulty accepting loss, bitterness, avoidance, and functional impairment.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| <25 | Normal grief | Supportive counselling |
| ≥25 | Prolonged grief disorder | Complicated grief therapy (CGT) |

- ≥25 has sensitivity 94%, specificity 98% for prolonged grief disorder.
- Concurrent high PHQ-9 = comorbid MDD → integrated treatment required.
