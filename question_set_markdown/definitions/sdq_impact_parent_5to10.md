# SDQ Impact Supplement (Parent 5–10)

**Domain:** Child Mental Health  
**Population:** Children 5–10; parent-rated impact supplement

## Description
SDQ impact supplement assessing whether any difficulties noted on the SDQ have caused upset to the child and interfered with everyday life. Covers home life, friendships, classroom learning, and leisure activities.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Impact Score | Impairment | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 | None–Trivial | Monitor |
| ≥2 | Significant | Priority referral; school liaison |

- Impact score independent of total difficulties score — high impact even with borderline difficulties = treat as abnormal.
- Functional impairment in school domain = coordinate with school wellbeing team.
