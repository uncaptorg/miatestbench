# Family Mental Health History

**Domain:** Family History  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Structured first-degree family psychiatric history including diagnoses, hospitalisations, suicide attempts, and substance use history.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Key clinical implications:
- **Bipolar disorder in first-degree relative** = elevated risk for bipolar spectrum; monitor mood carefully
- **Psychotic disorder in parent** = 10–15% lifetime risk for offspring; early psychosis monitoring
- **Family suicide history** = significant independent risk factor; explicit suicidality assessment
- **Parental substance use** = adverse childhood experiences likely; trauma-informed formulation

## Source
Standard clinical family history framework.
