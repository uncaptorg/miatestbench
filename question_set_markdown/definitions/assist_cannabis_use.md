# ASSIST — Cannabis Involvement

**Domain:** Cannabis Use  
**Population:** Adults (16+)

## Description
WHO ASSIST cannabis module assessing frequency of use, craving, problems, and dependence indicators over the past 3 months.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3 | Low | Brief advice |
| 4–26 | Moderate | Brief intervention |
| ≥27 | High | Specialist referral |

- High scores in adolescents (<25 years) = elevated psychosis risk; consider PQ-16.
