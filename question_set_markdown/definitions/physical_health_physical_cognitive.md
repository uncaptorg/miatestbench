# Physical Health — Physical and Cognitive Functioning

**Domain:** Physical Health / Cognition  
**Population:** Adults; Butterfly program

## Description
Assessment of physical symptoms (fatigue, dizziness, weakness) and cognitive symptoms (concentration, memory) in the context of eating disorders and general mental health.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Cognitive impairment in low-weight presentations often reverses with nutritional rehabilitation — do not over-pathologise
- Persistent cognitive symptoms post weight-restoration = refer for neuropsychological assessment
- Physical symptoms (dizziness, fainting) in eating disorder context = electrolyte check and cardiac review

## Source
NICE Eating Disorders Guidelines (NG69). [nice.org.uk](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng69)
