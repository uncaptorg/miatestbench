# SNAP-IV — Swanson, Nolan and Pelham Rating Scale (Parent/Teacher 6–17)

**Domain:** ADHD and Disruptive Behaviour  
**Population:** Children and adolescents 6–17; parent or teacher-rated

## Description
26-item ADHD and ODD rating scale aligned to DSM-IV criteria. Subscales: Inattention (9 items), Hyperactivity/Impulsivity (9 items), and Oppositional Defiant Disorder (8 items). Each item 0–3 (Not at all → Very much).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

**Subscale mean scores:**

| Mean Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| <1.0 | Normal | No concern |
| 1.0–1.5 | Borderline | Monitor; behavioural strategies |
| ≥1.5 | Elevated | ADHD assessment referral |

- **Inattention ≥1.5 + HI ≥1.5** = combined type ADHD likely.
- **ODD subscale ≥1.5** = oppositional defiant presentation → parent management training.
- Requires both parent AND teacher rating — pervasiveness across settings is a DSM criterion.
