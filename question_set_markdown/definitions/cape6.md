# CAPE-6 — Community Assessment of Psychic Experiences (6-item)

**Domain:** Psychosis-Like Experiences  
**Population:** General population and clinical (16+)

## Description
6-item shortened version of the CAPE-42, assessing positive psychotic experiences (unusual perceptions, ideas of reference, magical thinking, paranoia) in community and clinical populations. Each item rated on frequency (1–4) and distress (1–4).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Pattern | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| Low frequency + Low distress | Non-clinical experience | No action |
| High frequency + Low distress | Sub-threshold; monitor | Review at next contact |
| Any item + High distress | Attenuated psychosis risk | Clinical interview; consider UHR referral |

- **Frequency ≥3 on multiple items + distress ≥2** = refer for structured UHR interview (CAARMS).
- High frequency without distress may reflect cultural/spiritual experiences — contextualise carefully.
