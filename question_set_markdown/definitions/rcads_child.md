# RCADS — Revised Children's Anxiety and Depression Scale (Child Self-report)

**Domain:** Anxiety and Depression (Youth)  
**Population:** Children and adolescents 8–18; self-rated

## Description
47-item child self-report covering 6 DSM-aligned subscales: Separation Anxiety Disorder (SAD), Social Phobia (SocP), Generalised Anxiety Disorder (GAD), Panic Disorder (PD), Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD), and Major Depressive Disorder (MDD). Normed by age and gender.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

T-scores (age/gender normed):

| T-score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| <65 | Normal range | Monitor |
| 65–69 | Borderline clinical | Review; increase monitoring |
| ≥70 | Clinical range (98th percentile) | Diagnostic assessment; treatment referral |

- Subscale profile guides diagnosis: elevated SocP + GAD = anxiety disorder; elevated MDD alone = depressive episode; OCD subscale ≥70 = refer for CBT/ERP.
- Require both child and parent report where possible — discordance is clinically meaningful.
