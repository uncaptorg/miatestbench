# WHODAS 2.0 — WHO Disability Assessment Schedule (12-item)

**Domain:** Daily Functioning  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
12-item version of the WHO Disability Assessment Schedule 2.0. Covers 6 ICF domains: cognition (understanding and communicating), mobility, self-care, getting along (with people), life activities (work/household), and participation (community and society).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Simple Score (0–48) | Disability Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–12 | Mild | Psychoeducation; self-management |
| 13–24 | Moderate | Functional supports; rehabilitation referral |
| 25–36 | Severe | Intensive supports; disability services |
| 37–48 | Extreme | High-level care; NDIS consideration |

- Domain score analysis guides targeted intervention (e.g., high cognition domain → neuropsychological assessment; high participation domain → social prescribing).
- WHODAS scores can be converted to 0–100 using item response theory weights for research purposes.
