# BMC Sleep/Wake Cycle — PSQI + MCTQ

**Domain:** Sleep  
**Population:** Adults; CESPHN program

## Description
PSQI combined with the Munich ChronoType Questionnaire (MCTQ) to assess both sleep quality and circadian chronotype. Identifies social jetlag — the discrepancy between biological and social sleep timing.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **PSQI >5** = poor sleep quality → intervention.
- **Social jetlag >2 hours** = associated with metabolic disorders, mood disorders, and substance use.
- **Extreme evening chronotype** in adolescents and young adults = higher depression and anxiety risk.
- Chronotype mismatch with employment/school hours = occupational/educational performance risk.
