# DAR-5 — Difficulties in Anger Regulation Scale (5-item)

**Domain:** Anger  
**Population:** Adults (18+); NEAMI program

## Description
5-item self-report measuring problematic anger regulation including control difficulties, verbal and physical expression, and impact on close relationships. Scored 1–5 per item (total 5–25).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Severity | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 5–11 | Minimal anger problems | Monitor |
| 12–18 | Moderate anger dysregulation | Anger management; DBT skills; CBT |
| 19–25 | Severe anger dysregulation | Intensive intervention; assess risk to others |

- **≥12** = clinically significant anger problems.
- High DAR-5 + relationship difficulties = assess for IPV risk.
- Anger dysregulation in PTSD context responds better to trauma-focused treatment than generic anger management.
