# APA DSM-5 Level 1 Alcohol and Substances Screen (Parent 6–17)

**Domain:** Substance Use (Youth)  
**Population:** Children and adolescents 6–17; parent-rated

## Description
DSM-5 Level 1 substance use screening items from the cross-cutting symptom measure, covering alcohol, tobacco, cannabis, and other drug use for children and adolescents (parent report).

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | No use | No action |
| ≥1 | Any substance use endorsed | Proceed to ASSIST youth version or substance-specific Level 2 assessment |

- Any substance use in children under 12 = immediate clinical attention.
- Cannabis use in adolescents 12–18 + mental health symptoms = psychosis risk assessment.
