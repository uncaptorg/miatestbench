# SIDAS — Suicidal Ideation Attributes Scale

**Domain:** Suicidality  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
5-item self-report measuring attributes of suicidal ideation: frequency, controllability, closeness to attempt, distress, and interference with daily activities. Scored 0–10 per item (0–50 total). Validated as a treatment outcome measure.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–20 | Low | Monitor; safety plan if chronic |
| 21–44 | Moderate | Safety plan; increase session frequency |
| ≥45 | High | Crisis intervention; consider inpatient assessment |

- **≥21** = intervention warranted (validated cut-off).
- Item 3 (closeness to attempt) and item 1 (frequency daily or more) are highest-weight risk indicators.
- **≥5-point reduction** = clinically meaningful improvement.
