# Physical Health History (Butterfly Program)

**Domain:** Physical Health  
**Population:** Adults with eating disorders; Butterfly program

## Description
Physical health history adapted for eating disorder presentations. Includes specific eating disorder medical complications monitoring.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

Not scored. Key clinical flags:
- BMI <17.5 = severe malnutrition → medical stabilisation priority
- Syncope, chest pain, palpitations = cardiac risk → same-day medical review
- Dental erosion, Russell's sign = purging behaviour → dental referral
- Lanugo, hair loss, cold intolerance = severe restrictive eating → urgent medical review
