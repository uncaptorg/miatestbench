# Schuster's Social Support Scale

**Domain:** Social Connectedness  
**Population:** Adults; CESPHN program

## Description
Measures perceived social support across emotional, informational, and tangible dimensions.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- Higher = better perceived support.
- Low support on any dimension = targeted social support intervention.
- Low emotional support + depression = peer support programs, group therapy.

## Source
Schuster TL et al. (1990). *J Health Soc Behav*, 31(1):94–108.
