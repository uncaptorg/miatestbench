# SIDAS + C-SSRS — Combined Suicidality Assessment

**Domain:** Suicidality  
**Population:** Adults; AHS Summit program

## Description
Combined administration of SIDAS (Suicidal Ideation Attributes Scale) self-report and the C-SSRS clinician interview. Provides both patient-reported ideation severity and structured clinician risk stratification.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

- **SIDAS ≥21** = intervention warranted; ≥45 = high risk.
- **C-SSRS ideation level 3+** = active ideation with or without plan — safety plan and means restriction.
- **C-SSRS behaviour subscale any score** = prior attempt history — highest risk tier.
- Discrepancy between SIDAS (self-report) and C-SSRS (clinician) may indicate minimisation — explore further.
