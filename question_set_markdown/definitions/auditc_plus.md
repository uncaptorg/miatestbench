# AUDIT-C Plus — Alcohol Use (Concise + Supplementary)

**Domain:** Alcohol Use  
**Population:** Adults; CESPHN program

## Description
AUDIT-C (first 3 items of AUDIT) with additional supplementary questions on drinking context and impact. AUDIT-C measures alcohol quantity, frequency, and binge frequency. Supplementary items provide clinical context.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| AUDIT-C Score | Interpretation | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| <3 women / <4 men | Low-risk drinking | Routine health advice |
| ≥3 women / ≥4 men | Hazardous drinking | Proceed to full AUDIT or brief intervention |

- Positive screen = brief intervention using FRAMES model recommended.
- Supplementary items flag social harms and motivation for change.
