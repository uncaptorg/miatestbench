# K10+ — Kessler Psychological Distress Scale Plus

**Domain:** Psychological Distress  
**Population:** Adults (18+)

## Description
The K10 with 3 supplementary items on disability days and help-seeking behaviour in the past 4 weeks. Provides additional context for health service use and impairment beyond symptom severity.

## Scoring & Clinical Interpretation

| Score | Likely Status | Clinical Action |
|---|---|---|
| 10–19 | Likely well | Monitor |
| 20–24 | Likely mild disorder | Brief intervention |
| 25–29 | Likely moderate disorder | Mental health referral |
| 30–50 | Likely severe disorder | Urgent specialist review |

- Supplementary items: days unable to work/study, days with reduced activity, and service contacts in past 4 weeks.
- High disability days + low help-seeking = priority for outreach engagement.
