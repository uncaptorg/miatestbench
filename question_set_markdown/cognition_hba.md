# Question Set Representation

- domain: Cognition
- questionnaire: Cognition_Kildare

1. **Question**: Over the past five years, have you been concerned about your thinking and/ or memory?
   - code: HBA_1
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
2. **Question**: Over the past five years, have you been able to remember things as well as you used to? That is, is your memory the same as it was earlier in your life?
   - code: HBA_2
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
3. **Question**: Are there other aspects of your thinking that have changed over the last five years? (select all that apply)
   - code: HBA_3
   - options:
     - Slowed thinking when completing tasks 
     - Word finding problems (e.g. finding words in conversation, names of objects and familiar people)
     - Distractibility/ inattention
     - Getting lost in familiar places
     - Increased trouble with planning and organisation
     - More difficulty performing familiar tasks in home, social, and/ or work settings
     - Confusion about the time or place
     - Difficulties with problem-solving
     - Trouble understanding visual images and spatial relationships
     - Impulsivity and/ or disinhibition
     - Confusion in usual situations or environments
     - Poorer judgment than usual (e.g. making bad decisions)
     - Difficulty making decisions (e.g. impulsive or irrational decisions, unable to make a decision)
     - I have not experienced any of the above changes
4. **Question**: Do you think your thinking and memory skills are worse than other people your age?
   - code: HBA_4
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
     - Don't know
5. **Question**: Do these problems with thinking and memory interfere in any way with your day-to-day life?
   - code: HBA_5
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
6. **Question**: Have family members expressed concerns about your thinking and/ or memory?
   - code: HBA_6
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
