# Question Set Representation

- domain: Everyday Function
- questionnaire: CESPHN initial questionnaire [universal]

1. **Question**: Thinking about your ability to participate in everyday social, school (including university/TAFE) or work activities, how much of your _**usual activities have been limited?**_
   - code: SOFAS_IND
   - options:
     - Only everyday problems or concerns (eg. mild anxiety before an exam or an occasional argument with family members)
     - Only minor difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. difficulty concentrating after a family argument or temporarily falling behind in schoolwork)
     - Some difficulties with social, school or work activities but generally participates well and has some meaningful relationships (eg. occasionally skips school without permission or has difficulty sleeping)
     - Moderate difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. few friends or have conflicts with friends or co-workers)
     - Serious difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. no friends or unable to keep a job)
     - Major difficulties with several areas of social, school or work activities (eg. avoid friends or family and unable to attend school or defiant at home and failing at school)
     - Inability to participate in almost all areas of life (eg. stay in bed all day, or no job, home or friends or think about harming yourself)
