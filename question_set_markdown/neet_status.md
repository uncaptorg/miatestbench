# Question Set Representation

- domain: Unknown Domain (no-id)
- questionnaire: Butterfly_Initial Questionnaire

1. **Question**: Are you currently engaged in education or study (e.g. school, TAFE or university) on a regular basis? 
   - code: NEET_1
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes, enrolled in part-time study (includes waiting for term to (re)start)
     - Yes, enrolled in full-time study (includes waiting for term to (re)start)
     - Yes, enrolled in study but no longer attending
     - Yes, doing an apprenticeship/traineeship
2. **Question**: Are you currently engaged in paid employment or work on a regular basis? 
   - code: NEET_2
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes, part-time work 
     - Yes, full-time work
     - Yes, but absent on holidays, paid leave, on strike or temporarily stood down
3. **Question**: Are you currently engaged in voluntary work through an organisation or group on a regular basis? 
   - code: NEET_3
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
4. **Question**: Are you currently providing unpaid care, help or assistance to family members or others because of a disability, a long term illness, or problems related to old age on a regular basis? 
   - code: NEET_4
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes

12. **Question**: Thinking about your ability to participate in everyday social, school (including university/TAFE) or work activities, how much of your usual activities have been limited?  
   - code: AKV
   - options:
     - Only everyday problems or concerns (eg. mild anxiety before an exam or an occasional argument with family members)
     - Only minor difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. difficulty concentrating after a family argument or temporarily falling behind in schoolwork)
     - Some difficulties with social, school or work activities but generally participates well and has some meaningful relationships (eg. occasionally skips school without permission or has difficulty sleeping)
     - Moderate difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. few friends or have conflicts with friends or co-workers)
     - Serious difficulties with social, school or work activities (eg. no friends or unable to keep a job)
     - Major difficulties with several areas of social, school or work activities (eg. avoid friends or family and unable to attend school or defiant at home and failing at school)
     - Inability to participate in almost all areas of life (eg. stay in bed all day, or no job, home or friends or think about harming yourself)
