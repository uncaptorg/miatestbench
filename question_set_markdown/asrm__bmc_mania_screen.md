# Question Set Representation

- domain: Mania Like Experiences
- questionnaire: CESPHN initial questionnaire [universal]

1. **Question**: There are 5 statement groups below: read each group of statements carefully. Please note: The word “occasionally” when used here means once or twice; “often” means several times or more; and “frequently” means most of the time. Choose one statement below that best describes the way you have been feeling in the **past week** (cheerfulness).
   - code: ASRM_1
   - options:
     - I do not feel happier or more cheerful than usual
     - I occasionally feel happier or more cheerful than usual
     - I often feel happier or more cheerful than usual
     - I feel happier or more cheerful than usual most of the time
     - I feel happier or more cheerful than usual all of the time
2. **Question**: Choose one statement below that best describes the way you have been feeling in the **past week** (self-confidence).	
   - code: ASRM_2
   - options:
     - I do not feel more self-confident than usual
     - I occasionally feel more self-confident than usual
     - I often feel more self-confident than usual
     - I feel more self-confident than usual
     - I feel extremely self-confident all of the time
3. **Question**: Choose one statement below that best describes the way you have been feeling in the **past week** (sleep).	
   - code: ASRM_3
   - options:
     - I do not need less sleep than usual
     - I occasionally need less sleep than usual
     - I often need less sleep than usual
     - I frequently need less sleep than usual
     -  I can go all day and night without any sleep and still not feel tired
4. **Question**: Choose one statement below that best describes the way you have been feeling in the **past week** (speech).	
   - code: ASRM_4
   - options:
     - I do not talk more than usual
     - I occasionally talk more than usual
     - I often talk more than usual
     - I frequently talk more than usual
     - I talk constantly and cannot be interrupted
5. **Question**: Choose one statement below that best describes the way you have been feeling in the **past week** (activity).	
   - code: ASRM_5
   - options:
     - I have not been more active (either socially, sexually, at work, home or school) than usual
     - I have occasionally been more active than usual
     - I have often been more active than usual
     - I have frequently been more active than usual
     - I am constantly active or on the go all the time
6. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a definite period where for more than two (2) or three (3) days you felt much happier or more cheerful than usual?
   - code: AJE
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
7. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a definite period where for more than two (2) or three (3) days you felt much more self-confident than usual?
   - code: AJF
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
8. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a definite period where for more than two (2) or three (3) days you needed much less sleep than usual?
   - code: AJG
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
9. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a definite period where for more than two (2) or three (3) days you talked much more than usual?
   - code: AJL
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
10. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a definite period where for more than two (2) or three (3) days you were much more active than usual?
   - code: AKN
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
