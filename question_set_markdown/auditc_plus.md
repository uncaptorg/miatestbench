# Question Set Representation

- domain: Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test   Concise
- questionnaire: CESPHN_alcohol 

1. **Question**: How often do you have a drink containing alcohol?
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_1
   - options:
     - Never
     - Monthly or less
     - 2-4 times a month
     - 2-3 times a week
     - 4 or more times a week
2. **Question**: How many drinks containing alcohol do you have on a typical day when you are drinking?

One standard drink looks like this:
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_2
   - options:
     - 1 or 2
     - 3 or 4
     - 5 or 6
     -  7 to 9
     - 10 or more
3. **Question**: How often do you have six or more drinks on one occasion?
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_3
   - options:
     - Never
     - Less than monthly
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
4. **Question**: During the past three months, how often has your use of alcoholic beverages (beer, wine, spirits, etc.) led to health, social, legal or financial problems?
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_4
   - options:
     - Never
     - Once or twice
     - Monthly
     - Weekly
     - Daily or almost daily
5. **Question**: How old were you when you had your first full serve of alcohol?
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_5
   - options: none
6. **Question**: How old were you when you consumed enough alcohol to be ‘drunk’? 
   - code: AUDITC_MOD_6
   - options: none
