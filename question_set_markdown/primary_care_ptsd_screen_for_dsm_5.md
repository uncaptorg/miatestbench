# Question Set Representation

- domain: Post Traumatic Stress
- questionnaire: Primary Care PTSD Screen for DSM 5

1. **Question**: In the past month, have you had nightmares about the event(s) or thought about the event(s) when you did not want to?
   - code: PCPTSD5_1
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
2. **Question**: In the past month, have you tried hard not to think about the event(s) or went out of your way to avoid situations that reminded you of the event(s)?
   - code: PCPTSD5_2
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
3. **Question**: In the past month, have you been constantly on guard, watchful, or easily startled?
   - code: PCPTSD5_3
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
4. **Question**: In the past month, have you felt numb or detached from people, activities, or your surroundings?
   - code: PCPTSD5_4
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
5. **Question**: In the past month, have you felt guilty or unable to stop blaming yourself or others for the event(s) or any problems the event(s) may have caused?
   - code: PCPTSD5_5
   - options:
     - No
     - Yes
