# Question Set Representation

- domain: Unknown Domain (no-id)
- questionnaire: Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index

1. **Question**: During the past month, what time have you usually gone to bed at night?
   - code: PSQI_1
   - options: none
2. **Question**: During the past month, how long (in minutes) has it usually taken you to fall asleep each night?
   - code: PSQI_2
   - options: none
3. **Question**: During the past month, what time have you usually gotten up in the morning?
   - code: PSQI_3
   - options: none
4. **Question**: During the past month, how many hours of **actual sleep** did you get at night? (this may be different than the number of hours you spent in bed)
   - code: PSQI_4
   - options: none
5. **Question**: During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you cannot get to sleep within 30 minutes?

     - Not during the past month
     - Less than once a week

     - Three or more times a week
