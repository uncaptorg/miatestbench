   - options:

3. **Question**: Have you ever been told by a doctor or nurse that you have diabetes?
   - code: PHYSICALHEALTH_AGING_3
   - options:
     - Yes
     - No
4. **Question**: Have you ever experienced a major medical problem (e.g. cancer, heart disease, sleep disorder, etc.) that has affected your every day life?

   - options:
     - Yes
     - No
5. **Question**: Please specify major medical problem(s): Select all that apply:
   - code: PHYSICALHEALTH_AGING_5
   - options:
     - Arthritis, osteoporosis or other musculoskeletal problem
     - Autoimmune or endocrine disorder (e.g. lupus or thyroid disorders)
     - Cancer
     - Chronic pain
     - Dementia (e.g., Alzheimer's disease, Dementia with Lewy bodies, Frontotemporal dementia, Vascular dementia)
     - Epilepsy or seizure disorder
     - Headaches/migraines
     - Heart disease (e.g. atrial fibrillation, congestive heart failure, heart attack)
     - HIV or AIDS
     - Injury requiring hospitalisation (e.g. severe trauma)
     - Liver disease (e.g. hepatitis)
     - Neurological illness (e.g. MS, Parkinson’s disease, Chronic Fatigue Syndrome)
     - Renal (kidney) dysfunction (e.g. kidney failure, end stage renal disease, chronic kidney disease)
     - Respiratory problem (e.g. asthma, bronchitis, chronic lung disease)
     - Sensory problem (e.g. vision, hearing)
     - Sleep disorder (e.g. obstructive sleep apnoea, insomnia)
     - Stroke
     - Thyroid problems (e.g. hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, Grave’s disease)
     - Transient ischaemic attack or ‘mini-stroke’
     - Traumatic brain injury
     - Weight (e.g. obesity or underweight)
     - Don’t know
     - Other
