# Question Set Representation

- domain: Social And Occupational Function
- questionnaire: Work & Social Adjustment Scale

1. **Question**: Because of my mental health, my ability to work is impaired. '0' means 'not at all impaired' and '8' means very severely impaired to the point I can't work.
   - code: WSAS_1_
   - options:
     - 0 - Not at all
     - 1
     - 2 - Slightly
     - 3
     - 4 - Definitely
     - 5
     - 6 - Markedly
     - 7
     - 8 - Very severely
2. **Question**: Because of my mental health, my home management (cleaning, tidying, shopping, cooking, looking after home or children, paying bills) is impaired.

     - 2 - Slightly
     - 3
     - 4 - Definitely
     - 5
     - 6 - Markedly
     - 7
     - 8 - Very severely
